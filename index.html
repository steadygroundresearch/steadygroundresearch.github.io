Applied research that earns its place in the decision.

SG Research helps organisations use data, research, and analytical methods to identify opportunities, manage risks, and make informed decisions.

Mission

What we exist to do

Most organisations collect far more data than they have the capacity to fully explore. As a result, valuable relationships and insights often remain hidden due to limitations in time, tools, or specialised expertise. SG Research bridges that gap by applying rigorous, econometrically grounded methods to existing data, uncovering patterns and relationships that inform better decisions and improve performance.

The approach is built on techniques honed over an 18-year career in central banking, where the same methods were used to assess systemic financial risk at a national level. That rigor now applies more broadly — to any organisation tracking the same entities (customers, stores, products, employees, regions) repeatedly over time.

Value Proposition

The question we answer

"What does our information tell us — and how can we use it to make better decisions?"

The firm provides analytical expertise without organisations needing to hire a full-time specialist. The result is decision-ready evidence, not just dashboards.

Competitive Advantage

What we combine

Applied Data Analysis

Panel methods and econometrics on the data you already collect.

Economic Research

Rigor from an academic and central-banking research background.

Financial Sector Knowledge

Deep expertise in banking, credit, and systemic stability.

Practical Translation

Turning complex analysis into recommendations decision-makers can act on.

Long-Term Goal

Where this is heading

To become a trusted provider of applied research and analytics services for businesses and financial institutions — particularly in small economies, where organisations often need specialised expertise but cannot justify maintaining it internally.
