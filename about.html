About SG Research

Applied research, grounded in central banking rigor.

An applied research and analytics firm helping organisations use data, research, and analytical methods to identify opportunities, manage risk, and make informed decisions.

Most organisations collect far more data than they're able to fully explore — valuable relationships between variables often go unexamined simply due to time, tools, or specialised expertise. This practice exists to close that gap: applying rigorous, econometrically-grounded methods — including panel data analysis — to uncover the patterns already sitting inside data an organisation already has.

Mission To help organisations use data, research and analytical methods to identify opportunities, manage risks and make informed decisions.

The approach is built on methods honed over an 18-year career in central banking, where the same techniques were used to assess systemic financial risk at a national level. That same rigor now applies more broadly — to any organisation that tracks the same entities (customers, stores, products, employees, regions) repeatedly over time.

The result is decision-ready evidence, not just dashboards: statistically grounded answers to questions like what's actually driving a metric, which relationships are causal rather than coincidental, and how patterns evolve over time.

Available for remote research contracts and collaborative data analytics projects — across financial services and beyond.
